Sam Gao

UX Designer

Hi, I'm Sam!

I am a user experience designer who thrives on hearing people's stories. As a designer, I encourage people to reflect on themselves, others, and society through physical and digital artifacts. I hope to create meaningful interactions that will allow people to grow individually and understand one another at a greater capacity. I love thinkering, learning, and drawing. Outside of my work, you can find me dancing around the country.

I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University in May 2016 with a BFA in industrial design and human computer interaction, minor in architecture.
